Our Heartbeat

Faith. Fellowship. Coffee.

Three simple things that changed everything. Faith that anchors us. Fellowship that sustains us. And coffee that reminds us to slow down, sit together and actually do life with one another.

Christ and Coffee is what happens when women stop pretending they have it all together and start showing up for each other — open Bibles, honest conversations and yes, always a warm cup of coffee in hand.

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together." — Hebrews 10:24-25

Based in Connecticut — but building a community beyond borders.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Biblical Truth

Everything we do is rooted in the Word of God. We study it, live it, and grow through it together.

Authenticity

No masks. No performance. Just real women having real conversations about real life and faith.

Community

We believe women grow deeper when they do life together. You were never meant to walk this journey alone.

Growth

We celebrate progress over perfection. Every woman here is a work in progress — and that's exactly the point.
