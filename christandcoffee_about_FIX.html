How It Began

More Than a Bible Study

Christ and Coffee was born from a simple idea — that women grow deeper in faith when they do life together. What started as a small WhatsApp chat has grown into a thriving women's discipleship community built on authenticity, vulnerability, and the Word of God.

We gather twice a month for deep, discussion-based Bible study in a space that is vulnerable, honest, and welcoming. No perfect faith required. Just show up as you are.

What we didn't expect was how fast God would move. Women from different churches, different backgrounds, and different seasons of life found each other around a table — and something beautiful happened.

"Iron sharpens iron, and one person sharpens another." — Proverbs 27:17
Based in Connecticut — building a community beyond borders

Our Mission

Christ and Coffee exists to create a safe, authentic space where women can grow deeper in their faith, build meaningful community, and discover that they were never meant to walk this journey alone.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Biblical Truth

Everything we do is rooted in the Word of God. We study it, live it, and grow through it together — always with cross-references, real life application and honest discussion.

Authenticity

No masks. No performance. Just real women having real conversations about real life and faith. This is a space where vulnerability is celebrated not judged.

Community

We believe women grow deeper when they do life together. We are inter-church and inter-denominational — every woman from every background has a seat at this table.

🌱

Growth

We celebrate progress over perfection. Every woman here is a work in progress — and that's exactly the point. We grow together, stumble together and get back up together.

Why CC is Different

A Community Unlike Any Other

  • Christ and Coffee is a community-led, inter-church women's ministry, spiritually covered by Kingdom Life Christian Church.
  • Our discussions are vulnerable and real — not surface level or performative
  • We meet twice a month with a rotating schedule that fits busy lives
  • We go beyond Bible study — Temple Wellness, events, fundraisers and more

  • We are building a Kingdom Economy — supporting each other's businesses and callings

Meet the Founder

Melissa

Founder & Vision Leader — Christ and Coffee

Christ and Coffee was born out of a simple but urgent need — women longing for real community, authentic fellowship, and a place to grow deeper in their faith. When God put it on my heart to fill that need I was the first to question it. Me? I wasn't qualified. I didn't know enough. I had every reason to say no.

But God didn't call the qualified — He qualified the called. So I said yes. We started with 8 women around a living room table — open Bibles, honest conversations, and yes, coffee. That was 2025. Today Christ and Coffee has grown into a community of 47 women and counting.

One of the greatest blessings is that this ministry has never been tied to one church. Women from any denomination, any background, any season of faith are welcome here. That was never my plan. That was God's.
