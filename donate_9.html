Why We Do This

Built on Grace.
Sustained by Community.

Christ and Coffee has always been and will always be completely free to attend. No membership fees. No ticket prices for meetups. No cost to join. We believe that no woman should ever be kept from community because of finances.

But ministries have costs — venue fees, program materials, event supplies, website hosting and more. Every dollar sown into Christ and Coffee goes directly back into creating space for women to encounter God, grow in faith and find their people.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7

If God is nudging your heart — give cheerfully. If not — come anyway. You are always welcome here.
